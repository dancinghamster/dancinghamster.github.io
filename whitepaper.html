Dancing Hamster Whitepaper

Dancing Hamster was created as a concept after a playful spirit we all have to be creative and silly with Meme art. Created as a joke, it quickly grew adoption and a community hungry for more HAM. Thus the Dev set afoot on the journey to take Dancing Hamster to the moon. Further devolopment has caused HAM to go from a joke of a memecoin into a serious contender in the crypto market at large. The hamster was born to move and is learning new dance moves everyday. HAM is an adorably cute cryptocurrency made by a community driven to inspire joy and playfulness in the crypto space at large with memes, gifs, and hamster art.
Everybody loves to move along with the Dancing Hamster!

TOKENOMICS

Utility

Ham was designed as a peoples token with community voted decisions to take it into new directions of possibilites. As HAM gains more adoption and has higher esteem in the cryptomarket is when HAM will come out with community voted projects. For instance: video games, HAMswap, & possibly even NFTs.

Burning

No burning mechenism currently is in place for Ham. However any patrons who own HAM may send to the burn wallet as to reduce it from the maximum supply which would reduce the circulating supply. It helps maintain scarcity of the cryptocurrency and can increase its value.
